About Me

Hello! My name is Guillermo Barzana. I hold a Master's degree in Taxation and a Bachelor's degree in Accounting, and I am a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) in the state of Florida (license not active). With more than 35 years of experience in both private and public accounting, I've accumulated a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the field.

Throughout my extensive career, I have been dedicated to helping businesses and individuals navigate the complexities of financial management and compliance. Now retired from traditional accounting roles, I have decided to embark on an exciting new journey as a full-stack web developer. My goal is to leverage my deep understanding of auditing and accounting to create innovative, cloud-based audit software.

This software is designed to streamline the audit process, making it more efficient and accessible. Whether performing single audits or program-specific audits, my solution aims to provide comprehensive tools and features to meet the diverse needs of auditing professionals.

Thank you for visiting my page. I look forward to contributing to the future of auditing with modern, technology-driven solutions.
